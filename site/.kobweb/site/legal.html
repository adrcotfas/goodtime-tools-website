Privacy Policy

We Do Not Collect Your Data

Goodtime does not collect, store, or sell any of your personal data.

  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertisements
  • No user accounts required
  • No email addresses or personal information collected

Your Data Stays With You

All your data remains on your device. If you enable optional cloud sync:

  • Android: Data is stored in YOUR Google Drive (we cannot access it)
  • iOS: Data is stored in YOUR iCloud (we cannot access it)
  • Your control: You can delete this data anytime through your Google Drive or iCloud settings

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed by Google Play, App Store, and RevenueCat. We do not collect or store payment information.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Play/App Store: May collect information per their privacy policies
  • F-Droid: No Google Play Services if obtained from F-Droid
  • Website: goodtime.tools is a static site with no cookies, analytics, or tracking

Data Retention

  • Local data: On your device until you uninstall
  • Synced data: In your Google Drive/iCloud until you delete it
  • Purchase records: Retained by Google Play, App Store, and RevenueCat per their policies

Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EU, you have rights to access, delete, and object to data processing. Since we don't collect your personal data, these rights primarily relate to data in your own Google Drive or iCloud accounts, which you can manage directly.

Data portability: Your data is already in your control on your device and optionally in your Google Drive/iCloud. You can access and manage it directly from those locations.

Open Source

Goodtime is open source. Verify our privacy claims by reviewing the code at github.com/adrcotfas/goodtime.
